CUNY NYCCT Commencement Cover Design

01

For this project, I was tasked with designing two event covers for the CUNY NYCCT 85th Commencements and Academic Excellence Awards. This opportunity began with a client meeting, where I gained a clear understanding of the client’s vision and specific requirements for each cover.​​​​​​​

Over the course of a few weeks, I developed two polished designs, complete with professional mockups to showcase their real-world application. The final pieces were tailored to meet the client’s expectations, balancing creativity and formality to represent the spirit of the events. This experience not only refined my skills in concept development and execution but also emphasized the value of delivering high-quality work within deadlines.​​​​​​

REACHE Logo

02

For this project, I was tasked with designing a cover for PRINT Magazine, a leading publication in the graphic design industry with over 80 years of influence. The objective was to create a cover that appeals to a new generation of graphic designers while reflecting my personal expression and the community I identify with.Given the freedom to choose any subject for the cover, I selected sports, focusing on football and, specifically, Lionel Messi. As one of the most iconic figures in football, Messi represents excellence and inspiration, making him a fitting subject for this project. I incorporated imagery of Messi alongside some brief text about his achievements to complete the design.​​​​​​​
